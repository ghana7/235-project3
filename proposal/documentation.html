Process:

Beginnings

Our design process began with a discussion in Discord about what type of game we wanted to make. After discussing the idea of an idle game, we randomly generated a topic and decided to go with a farming idle game.

Design choices

The structure of the website was taken from Jimmie's project 1, with colors, fonts, and overall visual style changed to fit the new theme. We went with a brown and off-white color scheme, as is typical in farming-related media, and added some more vibrant accent colors for links.

Final polishes

I added some slight gradients on headings and menu bars for some added flair, made sure the page was responsive, and added in some images I made that will probably end up being assets for the game.
